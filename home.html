Classical music has a profound impact on modern society and culture. Its influence has shaped many of today's music genres. Musicians and composers use classical elements and blend them with their own personal styles. Many people enjoy attending operas and orchestra performances as it adds a story-telling element that cannot be matched by listening to it via a device. Classical music methods are also commonly used to portray emotional scenes in movies and other visual productions.
